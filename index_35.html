Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand usage patterns, and deliver relevant content. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information unless you have provided it to us.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are required for the website to function correctly. They enable core features such as security, session management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of essential cookies as the site cannot function without them.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies (via Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site — including pages visited, time spent, and traffic sources. This data is aggregated and anonymous. It helps us improve content and performance.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies track your browsing activity to deliver relevant advertising across platforms (Google Ads, Meta). These help us measure the effectiveness of our campaigns and ensure ads are shown to the right audiences.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages, including:

  • Google Analytics (analytics.google.com)
  • Google Ads (doubleclick.net)
  • Meta Pixel (facebook.com)
  • Cloudflare (cloudflare.com)

These third parties have their own cookie policies and we encourage you to review them.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. You can also opt out of analytics tracking via Google's opt-out tools.
